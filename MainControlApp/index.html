Nakhon Phanom University

The WaterPump Monitoring System is an IoT solution designed to automate and monitor water pump operations in residential and agricultural settings.

Key features include:

  • Real-time water level monitoring
  • Energy consumption tracking
  • Remote pump control
  • Automated operation based on water levels
